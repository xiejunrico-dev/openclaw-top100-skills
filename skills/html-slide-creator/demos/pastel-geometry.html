06 / 08

Features

Everything you need —
nothing you don't

Viewport fitting

Every slide fits exactly 100vh. No scrolling, ever — for you or your audience.

Inline editing

Press E, click any text, Ctrl+S to save. No code editing needed.

PPTX export

--export pptx via Playwright + your Chrome. Yours to keep.

Two-stage workflow

--plan outlines first. --generate makes the HTML. You review both.
