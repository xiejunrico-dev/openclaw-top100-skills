How It Works

From idea to presentation in minutes

01
/slide-creator
Answer 4 questions: purpose, length, content, images. That's the whole intake.
02
Pick your style
React to 3 visual previews. No describing design in words. Just point at the one you want.
03
Open and present
Arrow keys · Space · Swipe · Click dots. Runs in any browser. Forever.
06 06 / 08
Key Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Viewport Fitting
Every slide fits exactly 100vh. No scrolling. Content that overflows gets split — not squished.
Inline Editing
Press E. Click any text. Edit directly. Ctrl+S saves back to the file. No code. No IDE.
PPTX Export
/slide-creator --export pptx via Playwright + your system Chrome. Pixel-perfect output.
Two-Stage Workflow
--plan creates PLANNING.md outline first. --generate produces HTML. Review before you render.
