The Problem

"Presentations shouldn't require
a build system."

Complexity

npm install, webpack config, three hundred megabytes of node_modules — to ship a handful of slides. The tool consumes the task.

Sameness

Every AI-generated deck arrives identical: Inter font, purple gradient, zero character. Generic is the quietest form of failure.

Ownership

Closed platforms, export locks, monthly fees for your own content. That which you cannot hold, you do not own.

法
Process

Three steps.
One presentation.

Step I
/slide-creator

Type the command. Answer four questions. No configuration, no ceremony.

Step II
Choose your style

React to three visual previews. Your instinct selects more accurately than any description.

Step III
Open and present

Arrow keys. Space. Swipe. Pure HTML. No runtime, no cloud, no dependency.

能
Capabilities

Built for
real use.

Viewport Fit

Every slide fits exactly 100vh. No scrolling, no clipping — each layout is designed for the screen.

Inline Edit

Press E, click any text, Ctrl+S to save. Edit mode built into every presentation.

PPTX Export

/slide-creator --export pptx converts to PowerPoint when the boardroom demands it.

Two-Stage

--plan creates the outline. --generate produces final HTML. Review before committing.
