The Insight

Style discovery that actually works

"You can't describe design preferences in words."

I.
Ask mood
Tell it the vibe. Warm, sharp, playful, corporate-but-not-boring.

Generate 3 mini previews
Three distinct visual styles, side by side, ready in seconds.
III.
Pick the one that feels right
Trust your gut. That's the whole point.

The Process

From idea to presentation in minutes

I.
/slide-creator
Answer a few questions. Content, mood, audience, tone. Two minutes, tops.
II.
Pick from 3 previews
React to three visual styles side by side. No colour theory required—promise.
III.
Open your HTML
Arrow keys · Space · Swipe. Runs in any browser. No installs, ever.

Start to finish under ten minutes. We timed it. If it takes longer, blame your content—not the tool.
